Derivative of a constant function \\[ \frac{d}{dx} (c)=0 \\] --- ### 2. Power Functions \\[ \frac{d}{dx} (x)=1 \\] For an positive integer \\( n \\), \\[ \frac{d}{dx} (x^n)=n\cdot x^{n-1} \\] ### Remark Power function rule is true for all real \\( n \\) with a suitable domain condition. \\[ (\sqrt{x})'=\left(x^{\frac{1}{2}}\right)'=\frac{1}{2}x^{\frac{1}{2}-1}=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}} \\] --- ### 3. Constant Multiple Rule If \\( c \\) is a constant and \\( f \\) is a differentiable function, then \\[ \frac{d}{dx} \left[ c \cdot f(x) \right]=c\cdot \frac{d}{dx} f(x) \\] --- ### 4. The Sum - Difference Rule If \\( f \\) and \\( g \\) are both differentiable, then \\[ \frac{d}{dx} \left[ f(x)+g(x) \right] =\frac{d}{dx} f(x)+ \frac{d}{dx} g(x) \\] \\[ \frac{d}{dx} \left[ f(x)-g(x) \right] =\frac{d}{dx} f(x)- \frac{d}{dx} g(x) \\] --- ### Example 1 a. \\( \frac{d}{dx} (3x^4) \\) b. \\( \frac{d}{dx} (-x) \\) c. \\( \frac{d}{dx} \left( x^8+12x^5-4x^4+10x^3-6x+5 \right) \\) --- ### Example 2 Find the points on the curve \\( y=x^4-6x^2+4 \\) where the tangent line is horizontal. --- ### Remark \\[ (fg)'\neq f'g' \\] --- ### 5. Product rule \\[ \frac{d}{dx} \left[ f(x)\cdot g(x) \right] =\frac{d}{dx} [f(x)]\cdot g(x)+ f(x)\cdot \frac{d}{dx} [g(x)] \\]
